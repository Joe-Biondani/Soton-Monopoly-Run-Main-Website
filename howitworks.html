How it works

  • Start

    At the beginning of the day, you must check in at Guildhall Square. You will not be allowed to check in anywhere else until you check in there!

  • Find the check in places!

    You now have to go around central Southampton to find the check in areas!

  • Check in

    Once you have found a place, you must login to our website and check in!

  • Let that cash flow in! (or maybe out)

    As you run around checking in, you'll recieve cash! You can also lose it by visiting an already bought property or by buying a property!

  • Support

    Our team is here to support you throughout the day! If you encounter any issues, or have any questions, give us a call and we can try our best to help!

  • Leaderboard

    During the day you will be able to see where you are on the leaderboard! But, if you don't get to a place before the end of the day, you will have to pay 1.5x the normal rent! A couple hours after the event ends the results will be posted on the website.
