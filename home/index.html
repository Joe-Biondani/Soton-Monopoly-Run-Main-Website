We made it modern

We don't do our run in the typical paper trail, gosh no. We spruced it up a bit.

You get to play the game live by buying or renting properties, picking up chance cards and passing "Go" .

How our website works

We use a custom-made website to create a real-time competition factor as you go around the city!

From it you can view where you've visited, check in at locations via your device's geo-location and complete sections of our quiz!

You also get live updates to your location in our leaderboard, along with when other teams have visited your properties!

This is a not-for-profit event, and all money raised is put back into app development/maintenance, upgrading systems and general administrative services
