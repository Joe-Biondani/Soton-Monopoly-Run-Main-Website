About the team

  • Joe

    Core Management - Tech

    When it comes to the website or tech, Joe is the man. He's the one working behind the scenes to ensure the run goes as smooth as possible.

  • Isla

    Core Management - Team

    Isla is the one who does the jobs surprisingly people don't want to do! She also ensures everyone stays on-task during the day back at HQ whilst overlooking the whole run and seeing how we can improve.

  • Kim

    Game Master

    Kim is the one who decides all the locations and where you need to be! If you've seen something, she had to approve it first!

  • Sophie

    Social Media Manager

    Sophie is the one looking at all your messages and mentions throughout the day! If you have any questions, she'll be the one answering!

  • Amelie

    Support Line

    Amelie picks up calls and helps you guys check in/gives you tips if something goes wrong!

  • Steve

    Catering Officer

    Steve is probably the most crucial member of our team - refreshments.

  • Henry

    Team Manager

    Henry assists Isla in managing the team throughout the planning and running process.
